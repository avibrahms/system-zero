# Mass Market Engine

Own ONLY brand + customer acquisition. Everything else outsourced. AI handles operations. Gallagher/Medvi thin-architecture model.

**Target:** Medium-to-low sophistication consumers with primal unmet needs. Trigger their reptilian brain.

## Reptilian Triggers

Every product MUST hit 2+ simultaneously: Survival, Reproduction, Status, Fear, Comfort. Single-trigger → reject.

## 9-Gate Scoring (kill below 7.0 composite)

| Gate | Kill < | Question |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1. Market Desperation | 7 | Actively searching/complaining/spending on inferior? |
| 2. Reptilian Density | 7 | Hits 2+ primal triggers? |
| 3. Outsourceability | 8 | 100% outsourced creation/fulfillment/support? |
| 4. Unit Economics | 7 | Margin >55%? CPA < 1/3 first-order? |
| 5. Recurring Revenue | 6 | Consumables/subscriptions/refills? |
| 6. Speed to Market | 6 | Validation to sale <6 weeks? |
| 7. Ethical | 8 | Genuine value? No dark patterns? |
| 8. Market Timing | 7 | Trend accelerating NOW? |
| 9. Saturation | 7 | Supply absent or terrible? Trending = saturated. |

**Anti-saturation:** If trending on TikTok/podcasts/"best ideas" lists → auto-fail gate 9.

## Session Protocol

1. `python3 modules/solo-venture/engine/generate-ideas.py --bridge --quiet` (shared infra)
2. Check active validations — pull Google Ads performance, apply kill/scale criteria
3. After EVERY launch: `python3 modules/solo-venture/engine/venture-health-check.py --slug <slug>`

## International Gates (G10-G15, international entries only)

| Gate | Measures |
|------|---------|
| G10 Payment | Stripe-compatible? |
| G11 Fulfillment | Route exists? |
| G12 Language | AI-automated customer journey? |
| G13 Legal Entity | Cross-border or automatable entity? |
| G14 Margin After FX | >40% after conversion/duties/shipping? |
| G15 Market Size | ≥$1M/year addressable? |

Tiers: Domestic (US, G1-9, floor 7.0) → Tier 1 (UK/CA/AU, G1-12, 7.5) → Tier 2 (EU/JP/BR, G1-15, 8.0) → Tier 3 (CN/IN, G1-15+manual, 8.5).

## Principles

1. Validation before everything. 2. Outsource everything except brand. 3. Kill fast (14d validation, 60d revenue). 4. Scale winners aggressively (ROAS >2x = double budget). 5. Reptilian triggers only. 6. Speed is the moat. 7. Every failure = data. 8. Revenue is the only metric.
